Weight loss is a journey, not a sprint. Here are 10 tips to help you get started:

1. **Stay Hydrated**: Drinking water is crucial.
2. **Eat More Protein**: High protein diets can boost metabolism.
3. **Sleep Well**: Quality sleep is often overlooked but essential.
4. **Move Your Body**: Find an activity you enjoy.
5. **Manage Stress**: High cortisol levels can lead to weight gain.
6. **Eat Whole Foods**: Avoid processed junk.
7. **Read Labels**: Know what you are eating.
8. **Eat Slowly**: Give your brain time to register fullness.
9. **Use Smaller Plates**: A psychological trick that works.
10. **Be Consistent**: Consistency is key.
