# Why Hydration Matters

Water makes up about 60% of your body weight. Every cell, tissue, and organ in your body needs water to work properly.

## Benefits of Drinking Water

*   **Maximizes Physical Performance**: If you don't stay hydrated, your physical performance can suffer.
*   **Significantly Affects Energy Levels and Brain Function**: Your brain is strongly influenced by your hydration status.
*   **May Help Prevent and Treat Headaches**: Dehydration can trigger headaches and migraine in some individuals.
*   **May Help Relieve Constipation**: Constipation is a common problem that is characterized by infrequent bowel movements and difficulty passing stool.
*   **May Help Treat Kidney Stones**: Urinary stones are painful clumps of mineral crystal that form in the urinary system.

Stay thirsty... for water!
