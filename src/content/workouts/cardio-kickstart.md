# Cardio Kickstart (Beginner)

Build your cardiovascular base and improve lung capacity with this introductory steady-state program.

## Schedule
3-4 times per week, 30 minutes per session.

---

## The Program

### Intensity: Moderate (Conversational)
You should be able to speak a full sentence without GASPING for air, but still feel your heart rate elevated.

### Week 1-2: 20-25 Minutes
*   Walking at a brisk pace (3.5 - 4.0 mph)
*   Cycling on a stationary bike with light resistance
*   Swimming laps at a relaxed pace

### Week 3-4: 25-30 Minutes
*   Increase the pace slightly
*   Introduce 2-minute "power walk" intervals where you move faster than usual.

---

## Benefits
*   Improved heart health
*   Increased energy levels
*   Better sleep quality
*   Base endurance for more intense training later.
