# Core Dominator (Advanced)

Elite core training for maximum strength and aesthetic definition. These exercises require extreme stability and power.

## Structure
30 minutes, 3 times per week.

---

## The Elite Routine

### 1. Dragon Flags (or Eccentrics)
*   **Sets:** 3
*   **Reps:** 5-8
*   *The ultimate core challenge.*

### 2. Ab Wheel Rollouts (Full Range)
*   **Sets:** 3
*   **Reps:** 10-15
*   *Don't let your lower back arch.*

### 3. Hanging L-Sit Hold
*   **Sets:** 4
*   **Hold:** Max time
*   *Lock your quads and point your toes.*

### 4. Weighted Russian Twists
*   **Sets:** 3
*   **Reps:** 15 per side (Heavy)

### 5. Stir-the-Pot (on Stability Ball)
*   **Sets:** 3
*   **Reps:** 10 rotations each way

---

## Advanced Nutrition
*   Visible abs are the result of both training and a precise diet.
*   Maintain a slight caloric deficit to reveal the hard word you've put into your core.
