# Power Builder (Intermediate)

Focus on progressive overload and muscle development with this upper/lower split. Designed for those who have mastered the basics and are ready for more volume.

## Schedule
4 days per week (Upper A, Lower A, Rest, Upper B, Lower B, Rest, Rest).

---

## Upper Body Day (Example)

### 1. Bench Press
*   **Sets:** 4
*   **Reps:** 6-10
*   **Rest:** 2-3 mins

### 2. Pull-Ups
*   **Sets:** 3
*   **Reps:** Failure (or 8-12)
*   **Rest:** 2 mins

### 3. Overhead Press (OHP)
*   **Sets:** 3
*   **Reps:** 8-12
*   **Rest:** 2 mins

### 4. Bicep Curls & Tricep Extensions (Superset)
*   **Sets:** 3 each
*   **Reps:** 10-15
*   **Rest:** 90 seconds

---

## Lower Body Day (Example)

### 1. Barbell Squats
*   **Sets:** 4
*   **Reps:** 6-10
*   **Rest:** 3 mins

### 2. Romanian Deadlifts (RDL)
*   **Sets:** 3
*   **Reps:** 8-12
*   **Rest:** 2 mins

### 3. Leg Press
*   **Sets:** 3
*   **Reps:** 12-15
*   **Rest:** 90 seconds

### 4. Calf Raises
*   **Sets:** 4
*   **Reps:** 15-20
*   **Rest:** 60 seconds

---

## Progression Tips
*   Alternate between heavy days (lower reps) and volume days (higher reps) every 4-6 weeks.
*   Ensure 7-9 hours of sleep for optimal recovery.
*   Eat a protein-rich diet to support muscle repair.
