# Core Sculptor (Intermediate)

Add definition and strength with dynamic movements that challenge your abs from multiple angles.

## Structure
20-25 minutes, 3-4 times per week.

---

## The Circuit (3 Rounds)

### 1. Hollow Body Hold
*   **Hold:** 30-45 seconds
*   *Lower back glued to the floor.*

### 2. Russian Twists
*   **Reps:** 15 per side
*   *Rotate through your waist, not just your arms.*

### 3. Hanging Leg Raises (or Tuck-ups)
*   **Reps:** 10-12
*   *Control the descent.*

### 4. Side Planks with Dips
*   **Reps:** 12 per side
*   *Drive your hips high.*

### 5. Bicycle Crunches
*   **Reps:** 20 per side
*   *Slow and controlled tempo.*
