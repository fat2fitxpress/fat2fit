# Extreme HIIT (Advanced)

Push your metabolic limits with maximum intensity intervals. Only for those with high fitness levels ready to challenge their capacity.

## Structure
*   **Work:** 45 seconds
*   **Rest:** 15 seconds
*   **Total Rounds:** 5
*   **Target Duration:** 45 mins

---

## The Elite Circuit

### 1. Box Jumps (Height adjusted for challenge)
*   Step down to protect your Achilles tendons.

### 2. Plyometric Push-ups
*   Explode off the ground so your hands leave the floor.

### 3. Sprints (on Sled or Treadmill)
*   100% effort during the 45 seconds.

### 4. Renegade Rows
*   Engage your entire core while performing alternating rows from a plank position.

### 5. Tabata Sprints
*   Max effort for the final round push.

---

## Safety & Recovery
*   This is extremely taxing on the Central Nervous System. Limit to 2 sessions per week.
*   Focus on active recovery (walking/light stretching) the following day.
*   Advanced hydration with electrolytes is highly recommended.
