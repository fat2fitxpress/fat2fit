# Endurance Runner (Intermediate)

Improve your stamina and cardiovascular efficiency with these progressive running and interval sessions.

## Schedule
4 days per week (2 steady days, 1 interval day, 1 long day).

---

## Workout types

### 1. Steady State (30-40 mins)
*   Jogging at a consistent, challenging pace (Zone 2-3).

### 2. Interval Day
*   5 mins warm-up
*   6 sets of: 2 mins fast run / 1 min walking recovery
*   5 mins cool-down

### 3. Tempo Run (20 mins)
*   Running at a "comfortably hard" pace where you can only speak a few words at a time.

---

## Performance Tips
*   Invest in proper running shoes to prevent joint injuries.
*   Focus on a consistent breathing rhythm (3 steps in, 2 steps out).
*   Keep your shoulders relaxed and arms swinging naturally.
