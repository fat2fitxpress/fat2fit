# Cardio Elite (Advanced)

Elite-level endurance training for maximum performance. This program is designed for competitive athletes and extreme cardio enthusiasts.

## Schedule
5-6 days per week, combining long distance, intervals, and HIIT.

---

## Weekly Master Plan

### Long Distance Run (60-90 mins)
*   Focus on maintaining a steady target heart rate over a long duration.

### Fartlek Training
*   Unstructured speed play. Alternate between sprints, jogs, and walks based on landmarks.

### Speed Work (Track Session)
*   800m repeats x 6 with 90 seconds recovery.

### Recovery Cardio
*   45 mins of light swimming or cycling to flush out lactic acid.

---

## Advanced Metrics
*   Track your Resting Heart Rate (RHR) to monitor overtraining.
*   Monitor your VO2 Max progress.
*   Ensure high-carb intake on long distance days for glycogen storage.
