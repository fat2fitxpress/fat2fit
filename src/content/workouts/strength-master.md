# Strength Master (Advanced)

An elite powerlifting-focused program for maximum strength gains. This program pushes your limits with high intensity and specialized splits.

## Schedule
5-6 days per week using a Push/Pull/Legs (PPL) split or specific body part focus.

---

## Sample Leg Day (Maximum Intensity)

### 1. High-Bar Squats
*   **Sets:** 5
*   **Reps:** 3-5 (RPE 8-9)
*   **Rest:** 3-5 mins

### 2. Deadlifts (Conventional or Sumo)
*   **Sets:** 3
*   **Reps:** 1-3 (Heavy)
*   **Rest:** 5 mins

### 3. Bulgarian Split Squats
*   **Sets:** 4
*   **Reps:** 8-12 per leg
*   **Rest:** 90 seconds

### 4. Leg Curls & Extensions
*   **Sets:** 4 each (High Volume)
*   **Reps:** 15-20
*   **Rest:** 60 seconds

---

## Sample Push Day

### 1. Low-Arch Bench Press
*   **Sets:** 5
*   **Reps:** 3-5
*   **Rest:** 3-5 mins

### 2. Weighted Dips
*   **Sets:** 4
*   **Reps:** 8-12
*   **Rest:** 2 mins

### 3. Lateral Raises
*   **Sets:** 5
*   **Reps:** 15-25 (Burnouts)
*   **Rest:** 45 seconds

---

## Progression & Recovery
*   Use sophisticated methods like Deload Weeks and Block Periodization.
*   Focus on specific weak points in your main lifts.
*   Advanced supplementation and nutrition tracking are recommended.
