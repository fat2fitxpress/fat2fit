# HIIT Starter (Beginner)

Burn calories and boost your metabolism with this introductory HIIT program. Designed for manageable work-to-rest ratios and full-body engagement.

## Structure
*   **Work:** 30 seconds
*   **Rest:** 30 seconds
*   **Total Rounds:** 3
*   **Target Duration:** 20-25 mins

---

## The Circuit

### 1. Jumping Jacks
*   Focus on steady movement and proper breathing.

### 2. Bodyweight Squats
*   Sit back into your heels and keep your chest upright.

### 3. Push-ups (Modified ok)
*   Perform on your knees if needed to maintain correct form.

### 4. Mountain Climbers
*   Keep your core tight and "run" your knees to your chest.

### 5. Modified Burpees
*   Step back instead of jumping if you are just starting out.
