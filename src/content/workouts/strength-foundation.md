# Strength Foundation (Beginner)

Build a solid base with fundamental compound movements. This program is designed for newcomers to strength training to master form and build initial strength.

## Schedule
3 days per week (e.g., Mon, Wed, Fri) with at least one rest day between sessions.

---

## The Workout

### 1. Warm-up (10 mins)
*   Dynamic stretching
*   5-10 mins light cardio (walking or light cycling)

### 2. Main Lift: Barbell Squats
*   **Sets:** 3
*   **Reps:** 5-8
*   **Rest:** 90-120 seconds
*   *Focus on depth and keeping your chest up.*

### 3. Upper Body Push: Incline Dumbbell Press
*   **Sets:** 3
*   **Reps:** 8-12
*   **Rest:** 60-90 seconds
*   *Control the weight on the way down.*

### 4. Upper Body Pull: Bent-Over Barbell Rows
*   **Sets:** 3
*   **Reps:** 8-12
*   **Rest:** 60-90 seconds
*   *Pull towards your belly button.*

### 5. Shoulder Focus: Overhead Dumbbell Press
*   **Sets:** 3
*   **Reps:** 8-12
*   **Rest:** 60-90 seconds
*   *Keep your core tight to avoid arching your back.*

### 6. Core: Plank
*   **Sets:** 3
*   **Hold:** 30-60 seconds
*   **Rest:** 60 seconds
*   *Maintain a straight line from head to heels.*

---

## Progression Tips
*   Once you can easily complete all sets at the top of the rep range, increase weight by 2.5-5 lbs in the next session.
*   Focus on perfect technique before adding more weight.
*   Stay consistent and log your lifts!
