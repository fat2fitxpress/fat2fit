# Core Foundation (Beginner)

Build the essential core strength and stability needed for all other physical activities. This program focuses on safety and proper engagement.

## Structure
15-20 minutes, 3 times per week.

---

## The Routine

### 1. Plank
*   **Sets:** 3
*   **Hold:** 30 seconds
*   *Keep your body in a straight line.*

### 2. Dead Bugs
*   **Sets:** 3
*   **Reps:** 10 per side
*   *Push your lower back into the floor.*

### 3. Bird Dogs
*   **Sets:** 3
*   **Reps:** 10 per side
*   *Focus on stability, not height.*

### 4. Glute Bridges
*   **Sets:** 3
*   **Reps:** 15
*   *Squeeze your glutes at the top.*

---

## Why Core Matters
*   Reduces lower back pain
*   Improves overall posture
*   Provides the "anchor" for heavy lifting and explosive movements.
