# Protein-Packed Low Carb - Full Day Plan

## Breakfast
### Scrambled Eggs with Spinach
- **Ingredients:** 150g Eggs, 50g Spinach, 5g Butter.
- **Calories:** 300 kcal
- **Steps:** Whisk eggs, saut√© spinach in butter, add eggs and scramble.

## Lunch
### Grilled Chicken Caesar Salad (No Croutons)
- **Ingredients:** 150g Chicken Breast, 100g Romaine Lettuce, 15g Parmesan, 20ml Caesar Dressing.
- **Calories:** 450 kcal
- **Steps:** Grill chicken, toss with lettuce, cheese and dressing.

## Snack
### Cottage Cheese with Seeds
- **Ingredients:** 150g Cottage Cheese, 10g Pumpkin Seeds.
- **Calories:** 250 kcal
- **Steps:** Mix together and serve chilled.

## Dinner
### Steak with Buttered Green Beans
- **Ingredients:** 150g Sirloin Steak, 150g Green Beans, 10g Butter.
- **Calories:** 500 kcal
- **Steps:** Pan-sear steak to preference. Steam beans and toss in butter.
