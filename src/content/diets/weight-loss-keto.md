# Keto Weight Loss Mastery - Full Day Plan

This plan focuses on high-quality fats and moderate protein to keep you satiated while burning fat.

## Breakfast
### Avocado & Bacon Omelet
- **Ingredients:**
  - 2 Large Eggs (100g)
  - 2 Slices Bacon (30g)
  - 1/2 Medium Avocado (100g)
  - 10g Butter
  - 20g Spinach
- **Calories:** ~450 kcal
- **Prep Steps:**
  1. Fry bacon until crispy, then chop.
  2. Whisk eggs and pour into a buttered pan with spinach.
  3. Once set, add bacon and sliced avocado. Fold and serve.

## Lunch
### Grilled Salmon with Asparagus
- **Ingredients:**
  - 150g Salmon Fillet
  - 100g Asparagus
  - 15ml Olive Oil
  - 10g Garlic Butter
  - Lemon zest
- **Calories:** ~500 kcal
- **Prep Steps:**
  1. Season salmon with salt, pepper, and lemon zest.
  2. Grill for 4-5 minutes per side.
  3. Saut√© asparagus in olive oil until tender-crisp. Top salmon with garlic butter.

## Snack
### Handful of Walnuts & Celery
- **Ingredients:**
  - 30g Walnuts
  - 2 Stalks Celery (80g)
- **Calories:** ~220 kcal
- **Prep Steps:**
  1. Rinse celery and cut into sticks.
  2. Enjoy with raw walnuts for a crunchy, healthy snack.

## Dinner
### Beef Stir-fry with Broccoli
- **Ingredients:**
  - 150g Lean Beef Strips
  - 200g Broccoli Florets
  - 15ml Soy Sauce (Low Sodium)
  - 10ml Sesame Oil
  - 5g Ginger grated
- **Calories:** ~430 kcal
- **Prep Steps:**
  1. Heat sesame oil in a wok. Add beef and ginger, sear until browned.
  2. Add broccoli and soy sauce. 
  3. Stir-fry for 3-5 minutes until broccoli is tender.
