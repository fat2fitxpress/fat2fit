# Mediterranean Fat Burner - Full Day Plan

A balanced approach that emphasizes whole foods and satiety.

## Breakfast
### Greek Yogurt with Berries
- **Ingredients:**
  - 200g Greek Yogurt (0% Fat)
  - 50g Blueberries
  - 30g Almonds
  - 5g Honey
- **Calories:** ~350 kcal
- **Prep Steps:**
  1. Place yogurt in a bowl.
  2. Top with fresh berries and almonds. Drizzle with honey.

## Lunch
### Quinoa Chickpea Salad
- **Ingredients:**
  - 100g Cooked Quinoa
  - 100g Canned Chickpeas
  - 50g Cucumber
  - 50g Cherry Tomatoes
  - 10ml Olive Oil
- **Calories:** ~450 kcal
- **Prep Steps:**
  1. Combine quinoa, chickpeas, chopped cucumber, and halved tomatoes.
  2. Toss with olive oil, lemon juice, salt, and pepper.

## Snack
### Apple with Peanut Butter
- **Ingredients:**
  - 1 Medium Apple (150g)
  - 15g Peanut Butter
- **Calories:** ~200 kcal
- **Prep Steps:**
  1. Slice apple.
  2. Serve with peanut butter as a dip.
