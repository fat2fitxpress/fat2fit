# Plant Power High Protein - Full Day Plan

## Breakfast
### Tofu Scramble with Toast
- **Ingredients:** 200g Firm Tofu, 50g Spinach, 5g Turmeric, 2 Slices Whole Grain Toast.
- **Calories:** 450 kcal
- **Steps:** Crumble tofu, saut√© with spinach and turmeric. Serve with toast.

## Lunch
### Lentil and Vegetable Curry
- **Ingredients:** 150g Red Lentils (Dry), 100g Spinach, 100ml Coconut Milk, 50g Rice.
- **Calories:** 650 kcal
- **Steps:** Boil lentils and rice separately. Simmer lentils with coconut milk and spinach.

## Snack
### Hummus and Carrots
- **Ingredients:** 100g Hummus, 150g Carrots.
- **Calories:** 350 kcal
- **Steps:** Slice carrots and dip into hummus.

## Dinner
### Tempeh Stir-fry
- **Ingredients:** 150g Tempeh, 200g Mixed Veggies, 15ml Soy Sauce, 100g Quinoa.
- **Calories:** 750 kcal
- **Steps:** Pan-fry tempeh and veggies with soy sauce. Serve over quinoa.
