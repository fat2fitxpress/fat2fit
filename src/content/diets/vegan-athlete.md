# The Vegan Athlete - Full Day Plan

## Breakfast
### Buckwheat Porridge
- **Ingredients:** 80g Buckwheat, 200ml Soy Milk, 30g Walnuts, 1 Apple.
- **Calories:** 600 kcal
- **Steps:** Cook buckwheat in soy milk. Top with walnuts and sliced apple.

## Lunch
### Seitan and Broccolini
- **Ingredients:** 150g Seitan, 200g Broccolini, 150g Brown Rice, 10ml Sesame Oil.
- **Calories:** 800 kcal
- **Steps:** Saut√© seitan and broccolini in sesame oil. Serve with rice.

## Snack
### Dates and Nut Butter
- **Ingredients:** 5 Medjool Dates, 20g Almond Butter.
- **Calories:** 450 kcal
- **Steps:** Remove pits from dates and fill with almond butter.

## Dinner
### Pasta with Walnut Pesto
- **Ingredients:** 150g Chickpea Pasta, 50g Walnuts, 20g Basil, 15ml Olive Oil.
- **Calories:** 650 kcal
- **Steps:** Blend nuts, basil, and oil. Toss with cooked pasta.
