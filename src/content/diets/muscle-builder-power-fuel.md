# Power Lifter's Fuel - Full Day Plan

## Breakfast
### Steak and Eggs with Hash Browns
- **Ingredients:** 150g Flank Steak, 3 Eggs, 200g Potato (Grated), 15ml Oil.
- **Calories:** 950 kcal
- **Steps:** Fry hash browns until golden. Cook steak and eggs.

## Lunch
### Pasta Bolognese
- **Ingredients:** 150g Pasta (Dry), 200g Ground Beef, 150ml Tomato Sauce, 20g Parmesan.
- **Calories:** 1000 kcal
- **Steps:** Boil pasta. Brown beef and simmer with sauce. Combine and top with cheese.

## Snack
### Peanut Butter and Jam Sandwiches
- **Ingredients:** 2 Slices Bread, 30g Peanut Butter, 20g Jam, 250ml Milk.
- **Calories:** 650 kcal
- **Steps:** Make sandwiches and serve with milk.

## Dinner
### Whole Roasted Chicken (Portion)
- **Ingredients:** 300g Roast Chicken, 250g Mashed Potato (with butter/milk), 100g Peas.
- **Calories:** 900 kcal
- **Steps:** Roast chicken. Mash potatoes with butter and milk. Serve with peas.
