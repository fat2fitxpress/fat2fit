# Lean Bulk Evolution - Full Day Plan

## Breakfast
### Turkey and Egg White Scramble
- **Ingredients:** 100g Turkey Breast, 150g Egg Whites, 2 Whole Eggs, 1 Slice Whole Wheat Toast.
- **Calories:** 600 kcal
- **Steps:** Cook turkey and eggs together. Serve with toast.

## Lunch
### Chicken Breast with Quinoa
- **Ingredients:** 200g Chicken Breast, 150g Quinoa (Cooked), 100g Mixed Greens, 10ml Olive Oil.
- **Calories:** 750 kcal
- **Steps:** Grill chicken. Serve over quinoa and greens dressed with olive oil.

## Snack
### Greek Yogurt and Nut Mix
- **Ingredients:** 200g Greek Yogurt, 40g Mixed Nuts, 10g Honey.
- **Calories:** 550 kcal
- **Steps:** Mix all in a bowl.

## Dinner
### Lean Steak and Asparagus
- **Ingredients:** 200g Steak, 150g Brown Rice, 100g Asparagus, 10g Butter.
- **Calories:** 900 kcal
- **Steps:** Sear steak. Steam rice and asparagus.
