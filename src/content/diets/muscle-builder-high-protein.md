# The Bulk Master - Full Day Plan

## Breakfast
### Power Oats with Whey
- **Ingredients:** 80g Oats, 250ml Milk, 1 Scoop Whey Protein (30g), 1 Banana (120g), 20g Peanut Butter.
- **Calories:** 750 kcal
- **Steps:** Cook oats in milk. Stir in protein powder, top with sliced banana and peanut butter.

## Lunch
### Beef and Rice Power Bowl
- **Ingredients:** 200g Lean Ground Beef, 150g White Rice (Cooked), 100g Black Beans, 50g Salsa.
- **Calories:** 850 kcal
- **Steps:** Brown beef, mix with rice and beans. Top with salsa.

## Snack
### Protein Smoothie
- **Ingredients:** 1 Scoop Whey (30g), 300ml Milk, 50g Oats, 15g Honey.
- **Calories:** 600 kcal
- **Steps:** Blend all ingredients until smooth.
