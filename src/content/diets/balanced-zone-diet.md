# The Zone Diet - Full Day Plan

## Breakfast
### Scrambled Eggs with Fruit
- **Ingredients:** 3 Eggs, 30g Ham, 100g Grapes, 50g Melon.
- **Calories:** 450 kcal
- **Steps:** Scramble eggs with ham. Serve with fruit plate.

## Lunch
### Roast Beef Salad
- **Ingredients:** 150g Roast Beef, 150g Mixed Greens, 100g Kidney Beans, 15ml Olive Oil/Vinegar.
- **Calories:** 600 kcal
- **Steps:** Slice beef. Toss with beans and greens.

## Snack
### Almonds and Grapes
- **Ingredients:** 20g Almonds, 100g Grapes.
- **Calories:** 300 kcal
- **Steps:** Simple fruit and nut snack.

## Dinner
