# Standard Mediterranean - Full Day Plan

## Breakfast
### Shakshuka
- **Ingredients:** 2 Eggs, 150ml Tomato Puree, 50g Onions, 1 Slice Sourdough.
- **Calories:** 450 kcal
- **Steps:** Saut√© onions, add tomato. Crack eggs into sauce and simmer. Serve with toast.

## Lunch
### Greek Salad with Grilled Shrimp
- **Ingredients:** 150g Shrimp, 100g Cucumber, 50g Feta, 10 Feta Olives, 10ml Olive Oil.
- **Calories:** 600 kcal
- **Steps:** Grill shrimp. Toss with veggies and cheese.

## Snack
### Pecans and Dark Chocolate
- **Ingredients:** 30g Pecans, 20g Dark Chocolate (85%).
- **Calories:** 350 kcal
- **Steps:** Enjoy together for a rich snack.

## Dinner
### Lamb Chops with Couscous
- **Ingredients:** 150g Lamb Chops, 100g Couscous, 100g Grilled Peppers.
- **Calories:** 700 kcal
- **Steps:** Pan-sear lamb. Prepare couscous and serve with peppers.
