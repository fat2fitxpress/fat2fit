# Holistic Health Plan - Full Day Plan

## Breakfast
### Overnight Oats
- **Ingredients:** 60g Oats, 150ml Milk, 50g Greek Yogurt, 50g Berries, 10g Chia Seeds.
- **Calories:** 450 kcal
- **Steps:** Mix oats, milk, yogurt and chia. Refrigerate overnight. Top with berries.

## Lunch
### Turkey and Avocado Wrap
- **Ingredients:** 1 Large Tortilla, 100g Turkey Breast, 1/2 Avocado, Mixed Greens.
- **Calories:** 550 kcal
- **Steps:** Fill tortilla with ingredients and roll up.

## Snack
### Pear and Cheese
- **Ingredients:** 1 Pear, 30g Cheddar Cheese.
- **Calories:** 300 kcal
- **Steps:** Slice pear and enjoy with cheese.
