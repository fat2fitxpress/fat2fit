# Whole Food Plant Based - Full Day Plan

## Breakfast
### Berry Smoothie Bowl
- **Ingredients:** 100g Frozen Berries, 1 Banana, 30g Hemp Seeds, 200ml Almond Milk.
- **Calories:** 400 kcal
- **Steps:** Blend fruit and milk. Top with seeds.

## Lunch
### Buddha Bowl
- **Ingredients:** 100g Chickpeas, 100g Sweet Potato, 100g Kale, 30g Tahini.
- **Calories:** 600 kcal
- **Steps:** Roast sweet potato. Masssage kale. Combine with chickpeas and drizzle tahini.

## Snack
### Edamame Beans
- **Ingredients:** 150g Edamame (In Pod), Sea Salt.
- **Calories:** 300 kcal
- **Steps:** Steam edamame and sprinkle salt.

## Dinner
### Black Bean Burgers (Homemade)
- **Ingredients:** 150g Black Beans (Mashed), 50g Oats, 1 Whole Wheat Bun, Lettuce.
- **Calories:** 700 kcal
- **Steps:** Mix beans and oats. Form patties and bake. Serve in bun with lettuce.
